Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions about our services at Desire Mapping Technologies(Desmapp), covering digital marketing, web design, ad management, and more.

1. What services do you provide under Digital Marketing?

We offer a complete suite of digital marketing services, including SEO (Search Engine Optimization), SEM (Google & Bing Ads), Social Media Marketing (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube), Email Campaigns, Content Marketing, Influencer Collaborations, and AI-powered customer engagement solutions.

2. Do you provide website design along with marketing services?

Yes. We specialize in building high-performance websites — from simple portfolio sites to complex e-commerce and database-driven platforms — fully integrated with your digital marketing strategy for maximum ROI.

3. Are advertising costs included in your marketing plans?

No. Our plans cover strategy, creative design, campaign management, and optimization. Ad spend for Google, Instagram, Facebook, or other paid platforms is billed separately based on your budget.

4. Can you help me promote my business across multiple platforms at the same time?

Absolutely! We use an Omni-Channel Marketing Approach to run campaigns across social media, search engines, email, and other digital touchpoints — ensuring brand consistency and maximum audience reach.

5. How long does it take to see results from digital marketing?

It depends on your goals and the marketing channels used. SEO results can take 3–6 months to show significant growth, while paid ads and social campaigns can generate leads within days. Our team provides a clear timeline and monthly reports so you can track progress.
